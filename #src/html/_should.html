What should I know ?

Requirements to the borrower

A permanent place of work for at least 6 months and a stable income.

Our loan offer

The loan amount is up to 50,000 euros, the loan term is max 15 years, rates between 1.9% and 2.8% annually without change for the period of the loan.

It's easier than you think - proof of permanent income is enough.

— Preliminary examination lasts only 24 hours - we will inform you about the decision by e-mail.

— It is quick, without bureaucracy - it takes only 8 days from processing an application to issuing a loan.

Commission or other obligations

Only 1% commission on the loan amount (deducted from the loan amount after payment). No additional obligations, in particular, insurance coverage of losses is not required.

What is needed to apply for a loan?

If you are over the age of 18 (19 if a resident of Alabama or Nebraska, or 21 if you are a resident of Colorado or Mississippi) you are welcome to apply. Our application is completely online. Once the application is complete, your application will be processed. If anything else is needed during the verification process, we will contact you directly.

How can I apply?

We only accept online applications. To get started, go to eloan.com, fill the form and click on the "Get a loan" button.

How long until decision/funds received

Our goal is same day processing. Once a final decision has been made, you will be notified via email. The funds will be made available to your financial institution one business day after your approval, but it can take anywhere from 1-3 business days for your institution to process the funds.

What happens if I cannot pay my loan or installment on the due date?

If you have difficulties to meet the payment of a loan, you should contact the financial institution. If you think that you will not be able to pay a fee, or you are going to be late in paying it, you should contact them directly.

Financial entities will be willing to find you a solution, since they are interested in your repayment of the loan and they will do everything in their power to support you.
